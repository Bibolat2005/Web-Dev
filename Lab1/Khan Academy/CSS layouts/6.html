Almaty Arena

Microdistrict Nurkent Building 7, Almaty 050038
Ice Palace "Almaty Arena" is a new landmark of Kazakhstan. It was built in accordance with international standards specifically for the 2017 Universiade. It is located in the Alatau region, and the total area of the complex is 10 hectares. This is the largest sports facility in our country. It ranks second in size among similar facilities in the CIS and is the largest indoor ice rink in Central Asia.
